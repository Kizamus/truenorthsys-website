Contact

Get in touch.

For app questions, bug reports, feedback, privacy requests, or general messages about TrueNorth Systems, use the email address below.

Email

The best way to contact TrueNorth Systems is by email. Please include the app name, device or browser, and a short description if you are reporting an issue.

support@truenorthsys.app

Helpful details

  • Which app or page you are using.
  • What you expected to happen.
  • What actually happened.
  • Your browser and device type, if relevant.

Response expectations

TrueNorth Systems is an independent project, so responses may not be immediate. Messages are reviewed as soon as practical, especially reports about broken app behavior, accessibility problems, or privacy requests.
