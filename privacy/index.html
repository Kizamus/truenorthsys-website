Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

TrueNorth Systems builds web apps and tools. This policy explains what information may be collected when you visit truenorthsys.app or use a TrueNorth Systems web app.

Information you provide

If you contact TrueNorth Systems by email, your email address and the contents of your message will be used to respond to you. Do not send sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for your request.

Local storage

Some TrueNorth apps use browser local storage to save preferences or app progress. For example, the Sourdough Guide may store theme preference, loaf settings, current guide step, and completed checklist items on your device. This data is stored in your browser and is not submitted through a user account.

Advertising

This site may use Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. Advertising cookies help support ad delivery, fraud prevention, frequency capping, and measurement.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps at Google partner sites policy .

Cookies and browser controls

You can manage cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Blocking or clearing storage may reset saved preferences or app progress.

Third-party links

TrueNorth Systems may link to external services, including project subdomains and Google services. Those services have their own privacy policies and practices.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@truenorthsys.app.
